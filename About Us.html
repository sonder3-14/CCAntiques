OUR COMPANY

Our company and the brand, CC Antiques, was created with one objective in mind. That is making antique products and services easily accessible from the comfort of your own home.

OUR VALUES

We make our customers the highest priority for everything we do. Without them we wouldn't be where we are today. We always make sure they are treated accordingly and in line with our core values. Those values are respect, honesty and ethics. We make sure to exhibit those values when communicating, carrying out workplace tasks and providing services.

OUR PRODUCTS

Our products are usually found or given to us in a damaged or "ruined" state.
